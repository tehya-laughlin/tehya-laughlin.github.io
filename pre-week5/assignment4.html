Challenge 5: Accessibility Statement

For challenge 3, it's inaccessible because the inputs don't have ids or names that give them a description. Also, if the input names are't truely descriptive, say they are labeled like "checkbox1-3", or "text1" etc., then they aren't accessible either. Even if the descriptions are well done, then some of the inputs may not be accesible on their own. The color picker, for example, requires the user to see the color they are picking. And even if the hex was read out, then it may not give the user a clear idea of what the color is, like "red", or "blue."
